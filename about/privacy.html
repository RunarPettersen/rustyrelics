Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how Rusty Relics collects, uses, and protects your personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Name and email address upon registration
  • Profile information (bio, avatar, banner)
  • Activity data (bids, listings, wins)
  • Technical information such as IP address and device data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide access to your profile and account features
  • Enable bidding, listing, and transactions
  • Send service-related emails and notifications
  • Monitor platform usage and improve performance

3. Data Storage

Your information is stored securely and is not shared with third parties except as required by law or for legitimate operational purposes (e.g. hosting, analytics).

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your data at any time
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Submit complaints to data protection authorities

5. Cookies

Rusty Relics uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These may include functional, analytical, or session-based cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be communicated on this page with an updated revision date.
